About Me

I am a second-year at Northwestern University pursuing my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science. I intend on applying to Northwestern's Combined BS/MS Degree Program this year.

I'm also debating for Northwestern's Debate Team. Over the course of a season, I compete in multiple tournaments across the United States. The research that goes into preparation for these tournaments is equivalent to the work that goes into a Master's-level thesis. This past year, the research I did included areas such as the feasibility of AI alignment, the existing legal framework surrounding inventorship rights of generative AI, and the effects of vesting legal rights and/or duties into entities such as decentralized autonomous organizations, medical AI, and robo-advisors.

When I'm not working, you'll probably find me practicing piano, playing VALORANT and Counter-Strike, refining my speedcubing skills, or going to the gym.
